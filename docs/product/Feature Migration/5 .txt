The most important strategic insight

The strongest synthesis is not:

copy OIS_Cafe_v2 into VOIS v4

The strongest synthesis is:

keep VOIS v4 as the clean machine
and
restore OIS_Cafe_v2’s user-facing richness into it

That is the winning combination.

In plain words:

VOIS v4 = cleaner bones
OIS_Cafe_v2 = richer muscle and skin

You need both.
