Operating Standards
• Espresso Machine Operation
  • Purge group head before and after every shot to remove residual grounds and oils
  • Dose 18–20g for a double shot; tamp evenly at ~30lbs of pressure
  • Target extraction time: 25–30 seconds for 36–40ml yield
  • Backflush with cleaner at end of each shift; use water-only backflush every 2 hours during service
  • Report any unusual sounds, pressure drops, or channelling to shift lead immediately
• Milk Steaming
  • Use fresh cold milk; do not re-steam leftover milk
  • Target 55–65°C for lattes and flat whites; never exceed 70°C
  • Purge steam wand before and after each use; wipe with a damp cloth immediately
  • For oat milk: lower steam pressure, keep tip slightly deeper to reduce foam
• Station Cleanliness
  • Wipe counters and drip trays every 30 minutes during peak service
  • Keep knock box less than half full at all times
  • Clean grinder hopper and dosing chamber at end of shift
  • All surfaces food-safe sanitized before and after each shift
