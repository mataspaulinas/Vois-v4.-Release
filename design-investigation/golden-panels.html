Panel Rules

1.
Direction
Drawers open from the right, pushing Primary Canvas to the left.
2.
No fixed inspector
Drawers replace the inspector concept. Context is accessed by clicking links.
3.
Canvas minimum
Primary canvas never goes below 300px width.
4.
Sidebar auto-collapse
When canvas approaches minimum, sidebar collapses to 48px icon rail automatically. Reopens when space becomes available.
5.
Max drawers
3 content drawers + Copilot. If a 4th content drawer would open, oldest auto-closes.
6.
Close order
Right-to-left. Closing a drawer expands everything to its left.
7.
Copilot resize
Copilot's left edge is draggable. Can take up to 70% of viewport width.
8.
Identity
Each drawer has a 4px colored left border — purple (blocks), blue (tools), green (signals), red (failure modes), amber (response patterns), gradient (copilot).
9.
Ontology links
B105, T042, S008, FM003, RP007 etc. are ALWAYS clickable inline pills in any text throughout the app. Color-coded by type.
10.
Context passing
Copilot automatically knows which drawers are open and includes their content as context.
11.
Mobile
On mobile (<768px), drawers open as full-screen sheets with back navigation. No side-by-side.
12.
Keyboard
Esc = close rightmost drawer. Cmd+\ = toggle sidebar. Cmd+K = command palette.
